What is proxy? 

Proxies have already been explained on the Reverse Proxy article.

What is forward proxy? 

Forward proxies are used by the clients to protect their identities. Usually that applies to a group of users from a company, that sends requests to the internet through the forward proxy with administrator control.

Forward proxies seems to be a lot less commom than Reverse proxy (The former has 116k results on Google and the latter, 2.910k).
